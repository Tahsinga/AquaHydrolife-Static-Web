Our Services

Comprehensive borehole drilling solutions tailored to your water needs

Water Survey

Detailed water resource assessments and hydrogeological surveys to identify reliable sources and ensure successful projects.

Borehole Siting

Professional siting using geological and geophysical techniques to determine optimal borehole locations.

Flushing & Rehabilitation

Well flushing and rehabilitation services to restore flow and water quality in existing boreholes.

Bush Pumps & Water Pumps

Supply, installation and maintenance of bush pumps and modern water pumps for all applications.

Water Tanks

Provision and installation of water storage solutions to match your demand and ensure reliable supply.

Solar Borehole Installation

Design and installation of solar-powered borehole systems for sustainable, off-grid water supply.
